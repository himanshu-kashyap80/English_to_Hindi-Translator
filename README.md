# English_to_Hindi-Translator

he English-to-Hindi Translator for Government Websites is a project designed to make government information more accessible to Hindi-speaking citizens. This tool automatically translates English content from government portals into Hindi, helping to overcome language barriers.

It ensures that important details about government schemes, health advisories, legal notices, and educational materials are easily understood by Hindi readers. The project supports the goals of Digital India and e-Governance, promoting inclusivity and transparency in government communication.
